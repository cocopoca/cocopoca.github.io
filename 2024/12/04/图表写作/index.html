经济，技术，政策，文化

  1. 经济 (Economy)

    • 词汇：Economy, Economic
    • 常用表达：
      • The Chinese economy has shown strong growth over the past decade.
      • Economic policies have a significant impact on market trends.
      • The global economy is increasingly interconnected.

  2. 技术 (Technology)

    • 词汇：Technology, Technological
    • 常用表达：
      • Advances in technology have revolutionized many industries.
      • Technological innovation is driving economic development.
      • The rapid pace of technological change presents both opportunities and challenges.

  3. 政策 (Policy)

    • 词汇：Policy, Policies
    • 常用表达：
      • Government policies play a crucial role in shaping the business environment.
      • Environmental policies aim to reduce pollution and promote sustainability.
      • Economic policies are designed to stimulate growth and stability.

  4. 文化 (Culture)

    • 词汇：Culture, Cultural
    • 常用表达：
      • Chinese culture has a rich history and diverse traditions.
      • Cultural exchange promotes mutual understanding between different countries.
      • The influence of popular culture can be seen in various aspects of daily life.

两个句子中使用 “when” 的情况不同，主要是因为它们所描述的关系和动作的性质不同。让我们来分析一下这两个句子：

  1. 关于顾客选择餐厅的因素：

    • “The pie chart illustrates the different factors that customers in a certain city considered when choosing a restaurant in 2017.”
    • 在这个句子中，”when” 用来连接两个事件：顾客考虑某些因素（the different factors that customers considered）和他们选择餐厅的动作（choosing a restaurant）。这里 “when” 表示的是时间点或时间段，即在顾客做选择的那个时刻，他们在考虑这些因素。

  2. 关于学生使用手机阅读的目的：

    • “The pie chart illustrates the different purposes for which students at a certain university used their phones for reading in 2017.”
    • 在这个句子中，”for which” 是用来引导定语从句，修饰 “purposes”。这里的重点是学生使用手机阅读的具体目的，而不是在特定的时间点上发生的动作。因此，不需要用 “when” 来表示时间关系。”in 2017” 则是用来限定这一行为发生的时间范围，但不是为了表示某个具体的选择时刻。

总结来说，第一个句子中的 “when” 强调了在做出选择时考虑的因素，而第二个句子中的 “for which” 更关注于行为的目的。如果在第二个句子中使用 “when”，则可能会改变句子的意思，暗示学生是在特定的时间点决定使用手机阅读，这并不是句子想要传达的主要信息。

使用 “for which”

  • 先行词是“目的”或“原因”：
    • “The reason for which we exercise is to stay healthy.”
    • “The purpose for which we exercise is to improve our fitness.”

使用 “that”

  • 先行词是“人”或“物”：
    • “The book that I read last night was very interesting.”
    • “The person that I met at the party was very friendly.”
  • 先行词是“方式”或“方法”：
    • “The method that we use to solve the problem is efficient.”
    • “The way that we organize our tasks is crucial.”

使用 “which”

  • 先行词是“物”：
    • “The car which I bought last year is very reliable.”
    • “The house which we live in is quite spacious.”

使用 “in which”

  • 先行词是“方式”或“方法”：
    • “The way in which we solve problems is important.”
    • “The method in which we conduct our experiments is crucial.”

使用 “that which”

  • 先行词是“物”或“概念”：
    • “That which is most important is often overlooked.”
    • “That which we seek is often hidden.”

为什么 “By” 和 “That” 不能互换

  • 语法功能不同：”By” 是介词，通常跟在动词后面，表示方式、手段、代理等；而 “that” 是连词或关系代词，用于引导从句或指代事物。
  • 语义不同：”By” 侧重于描述动作的方式、时间和地点，而 “that” 用于连接句子部分或指代特定的事物。
  • 句子结构不同：”By” 通常出现在句中或句尾，作为介词短语的一部分；而 “that” 通常出现在句中，引导从句或作为指示代词。

4. 举例说明

  • 正确用法：

    • Using “by”:

      The project was completed by the team.

      • Explanation: “by” 表示项目的完成者是团队。

    • Using “that”:

      The project that was completed by the team was highly successful.

      • Explanation: “that” 引导定语从句，修饰 “project”，指明被团队完成的那个项目。

  • 错误替换：

    • Incorrect: The project was completed that the team.
      • Explanation: 这里的 “that” 不能替代 “by”，因为 “that” 不能表示代理或执行者。
    • Incorrect: The project by was completed by the team.
      • Explanation: 这里的 “by” 不能替代 “that”，因为 “by” 不能引导定语从句。
